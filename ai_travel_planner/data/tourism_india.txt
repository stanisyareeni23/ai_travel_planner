OA – Travel Guide
Overview

Goa is a coastal state in western India known for beaches, nightlife, Portuguese heritage, churches, and water sports. Suitable for couples, friends, solo travelers, and party tourism.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal. Monsoon (June–Sept) is lush but limits water sports.

Top Places

Baga & Calangute – nightlife, parasailing

Anjuna & Vagator – sunset, flea market

Old Goa – Basilica of Bom Jesus, Se Cathedral

Panaji – Latin Quarter, cafes

Dudhsagar Falls – waterfalls

7-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Calangute → Baga → Tito’s Lane
Day 2: Anjuna → Vagator → Chapora Fort
Day 3: Old Goa churches → Panaji
Day 4: Dudhsagar → Spice plantation
Day 5: South Goa (Colva, Benaulim)
Day 6: Water sports + shopping
Day 7: Relaxation + return

Expenses (per person approx)

Accommodation: ₹1200–2500/night
Food: ₹800/day
Local transport: ₹400/day
Activities: ₹2000 total

Total for 7 days: ₹18,000–₹25,000

Tips

Rent scooter for mobility

Avoid unsafe beaches at night

Book hotels early in Dec–Jan

KERALA – Travel Guide
Overview

Kerala is known for backwaters, tea hills, beaches, Ayurveda, and wildlife.

Best Time to Visit

October–March. Monsoon for Ayurveda tourism.

Top Places

Munnar – tea gardens

Thekkady – Periyar sanctuary

Alleppey – houseboats

Kochi – colonial heritage

Varkala – cliff beaches

7-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Munnar
Day 2: Munnar sightseeing
Day 3: Thekkady
Day 4: Alleppey houseboat
Day 5: Kochi
Day 6: Varkala
Day 7: Relax + return

Expenses

Accommodation: ₹1500/night
Houseboat: ₹6000 (shared)
Food: ₹700/day
Transport: ₹500/day

Total: ₹22,000–₹30,000

Tips

Book houseboats early

Carry mosquito repellent

Light cotton clothes
